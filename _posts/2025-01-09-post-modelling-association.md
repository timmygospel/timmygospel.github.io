When we think about machine learning, we often imagine models that **predict outcomes** — prices, labels, or categories.

But not all machine learning is about prediction.

**Association learning** is a type of *unsupervised learning* that focuses on **discovering hidden relationships** inside data — without any labels.

In this article, we’ll explore what association learning is, how it works, and why it’s widely used in areas like recommendations and market basket analysis.
